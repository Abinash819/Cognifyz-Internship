Eclipse

An eclipse occurs when one celestial body moves into the shadow of another. It's a captivating astronomical event that can be observed from various parts of the world. Solar eclipses happen when the Moon passes between the Earth and the Sun, blocking sunlight. Lunar eclipses occur when the Earth moves between the Sun and the Moon, casting a shadow on the Moon. These awe-inspiring phenomena have intrigued and inspired humans for centuries, offering unique opportunities for scientific study and cultural interpretation.
